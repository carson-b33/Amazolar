Who We Are

We are an emerging nonprofit initiative seeking to partner with under-resourced schools in Ecuador, to support teachers and empower students through the transformative power of solar energy.

Our Process

The Amazolar Path

A four-step journey from identifying communities in need to delivering sustainable, solar-powered solutions.

Step 01
01
Community Discovery

We identify under-resourced schools in Ecuador that lack access to reliable energy and quality learning environments.

Step 02
02
Partner Engagement

Our team visits schools in person, builds relationships with educators, administrators, and local communities.

Step 03
03
Asset Mapping

We conduct thorough assessments to determine the areas of greatest need and identify the highest-impact opportunities.

Step 04
04
Impact in Action

We provide solar resources and ongoing support to create sustainable, long-term impact for students and teachers alike.
